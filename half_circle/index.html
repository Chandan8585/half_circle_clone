How we Work

Visualizing the end product at very initial stage is vital for achieving it. The most important part is to gather the information from you and then organizing the resources to achieve the target result within a time frame. Post brain storming, we divide the project into stages and assign it to various expert teams so that they can work on it independently and take it to the next level. Finally, you get desired results and product. Usually, we divide the project into four stages: 1). Understanding the idea 2). Rolling out the pan 3). Create 4). Deliver.

At this very first stage we understand your requirement and do the SWOT analysis. Time, technique and budget are the main considerations. We document each and every part of the requirement in detail. Everything becomes quite clear at this stage and help in next level planning.

A proper plan is required to achieve the desired result on time. At this second stage, we chalk out detailed plan and allocate the resources accordingly. This planning helps to avoid mismanagement and ensure timely delivery of the task.

After planning we assign it to various teams. This is very crucial stage of the journey. First a design is created and then the working starts to deliver the final product. We keep you informed about the progress. At the end we deliver the quality product.

This is final stage of the life cycle of a project or a task. Before going for final delivery, internal team tests and analyse the work and recheck everything. Each point is matched with the documentation done at first stage. If everything is as per plan, we deliver it to you. If it is SEO or digital marketing project, we send the reports of the same to you.

What We Do

Digital Marketing

To propel your business and establish it as a well known brand, we provide all direction digital marketing services. We are cost effective and enable you to reach potential customers using the internet and other forms of digital communication.

Content Creation

In this digital world, individual popularity and business brand popularity depends on the content you create to engage the audience. We create viral contents for social media, website as well as for films, documentaries termed as story boarding.

Promotional Services

We provide promotional services for business brands for their services and product. We also serve individuals, political party and people’s representatives. We have specialized services on promotion for start-ups that increases their reach to the customer in a very short span of time.

Political Services

A political party will prevail over his rivals only if it has right strategy based on ground reality. There is a cut-throat competition in politics. We help political parties and politicians in their campaign management, surveys, digital promotion, social media management as well as giving insights in the creation of posters, banners and hoardings.

Video Creation

Create, share and repeat is the advantage that a video provides. Whether it is a political event or a corporate one or a event in family, we provide 360 degree video production.

Photography

A traditional, popular, durable way to capture a moment is the photography. Whether it is an event or a product, we provide all the service managed by our professional lensmen.
